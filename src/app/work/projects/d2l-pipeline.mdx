## Overview

D2L Pipeline tackles a messy but valuable problem: turning documents into structured material that can be used for LoRA training and downstream model adaptation. It is an automation-heavy workflow where consistency matters more than a flashy interface.

## Key Components

- **Document normalization and extraction**
- **Repeatable preprocessing steps**
- **Pipeline automation across multiple stages**
- **Python and TypeScript orchestration where each layer fits best**

## Why It Matters

This project reflects how I approach AI work in practice. Useful AI systems are rarely just prompts. They are pipelines, validation steps, transformations, and operational glue that make the model usable in a real environment.

## Outcome

D2L Pipeline shows experience beyond surface-level app work. It demonstrates comfort with the data and automation layers that usually determine whether an AI initiative compounds or collapses.
