## Overview

RRA is a retrieval-augmented generation application focused on turning internal knowledge into a usable chat interface. It handles the unglamorous but necessary work: ingestion, chunking, retrieval quality, and UI behavior that makes answers useful.

## Core Work

- **Document ingestion pipelines**
- **Retrieval tuning and response grounding**
- **Conversation UX for knowledge access**
- **Production deployment considerations** instead of demo-only behavior

## Why It Matters

Many RAG projects stop at a prototype. RRA pushed further into the production concerns teams actually face: data freshness, response quality, and whether the chat experience helps a real user complete work faster.

## Outcome

RRA gave me a working base for internal knowledge systems and sharpened the implementation patterns I now bring into fractional engagements that need reliable AI features inside existing products.
