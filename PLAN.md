# Fractional CTO/AI Engineer — Research & ryanlisse.com Plan

## 📊 BEADS: Background

### Market Overview (2026)
**Fractional CTO** = part-time C-level technical leadership for startups/scaleups that can't justify a $250K+ full-time hire.

**The Scale-Up Gap**: Too big to run without technical leadership, too small for a full-time executive. The fractional model is now the default for Series A and bootstrapped SaaS companies.

### Rates

| Tier | Hourly | Monthly Retainer | Best For |
|------|--------|-----------------|----------|
| Advisory | $200–300/hr | $4–6K/mo (~2-4h/wk) | Solo founders needing guidance |
| Standard | $300–400/hr | $8–12K/mo (~5-10h/wk) | Managing small dev teams |
| Executive | $400–600/hr | $15–25K/mo (~15-20h/wk) | Series A, strict deadlines |
| Sprint/Kickstart | — | $7–10K/week | Intensive 1-week deep dives |

**AI-specialized fractional CTOs charge 20-40% premium** over general tech due to narrower talent pool.

### Key Platforms
- **fractional.ai** — Elite, enterprise GenAI (Change.org, Airbyte case studies)
- **fractionaljobs.io** — Largest job board (~$10K/mo listings)
- **fractionalsunited.com** — Peer community (Karina Mikhli)
- **fractionalctos.org** — Top 20 directory + educational content
- **Angel.co / Wellfound** — Startup job board with fractional roles
- **LinkedIn** — Still #1 for referral-driven discovery

### Discovery Channels (from Reddit/community research)
1. **Referrals** — #1 channel by far. Every fractional CTO says this.
2. **SEO/Content** — Blog posts ranking for "fractional CTO [niche]"
3. **LinkedIn thought leadership** — Weekly posts about AI architecture decisions
4. **Portfolio website** — Case studies demonstrating outcomes
5. **Communities** — fractionalsunited, Indie Hackers, relevant Discords

### Competitor Example: stevens.dev
"Fractional CTO & AI Engineer" — clean positioning, project showcase, production-grade AI focus. Good reference for structure.

| **Builds vs Advises** | Ships production code (60+ repos) | Draws architecture diagrams |

### Positioning Decision

**"Fractional AI Engineer / Agent Architect"** > "Fractional CTO"

Why:
- "CTO" implies management/strategy — Ryan builds
- "AI Engineer" signals hands-on production code
- "Agent Architect" is a differentiated niche (almost nobody claims this)
- Combines builder + architect credibility

**Tagline options:**
1. "I build AI agents that work while you sleep"
2. "Fractional AI Engineer — from prototype to production in weeks, not quarters"
3. "Your startup's AI co-founder, without the co-founder drama"
